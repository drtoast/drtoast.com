(Briefly: it was accidentally invented in 1491 by an obscure French alchemist named Gerard Depardieu, who was actually trying to turn bread into gold. He also had to invent plastic and electricity.)

In 2008, the Chinese version of the Wikipedia article on toast cited this research as fact. It remained on Wikipedia for over two years before it was finally removed by overzealous "truthists."
