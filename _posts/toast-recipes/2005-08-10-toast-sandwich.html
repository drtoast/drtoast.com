--- layout: post title: Toast Sandwich wordpress_id: 57 wordpress_url: http://www.drtoast.com/crumbs/57 date: 2005-08-10 03:03:01.000000000 -07:00 categories: - toast-recipes --- from Colin Panisset (Colin.Panisset@Sun.COM) I've got another recipe for you. This one comes from "Mrs. Beaton's Book of Household Management", which dates from sometime in the 19th century, I think. ---- Begin ---- Toast Sandwich Ingredients: 2 thick slices soft white bread 1 thin slice slightly stale bread salt and pepper Method: Toast the thin slice to an even golden brown on both sides. Place it between the two thick slices and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serves 1. ---- End ---- Bizarrely, this actually tastes pretty good.
