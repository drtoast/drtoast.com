--- layout: post title: Toast Burger wordpress_id: 61 wordpress_url: http://www.drtoast.com/crumbs/61 date: 2005-08-10 03:04:50.000000000 -07:00 categories: - toast-recipes --- from Richard Purdie (RPURDIE@clear.co.nz) Sesame Seed Buns Water get two sesame seed buns. Grill and add lashings of butter. Get another bun, grill both sides to golden brown, sprinkle with salt and pepper and place between the other buns. Serve half cold in a paper bag , on a tray with a coke and fries. Smile a lot.
