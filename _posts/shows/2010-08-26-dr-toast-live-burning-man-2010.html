August 30-September 6, 2010: Dr. Toast at Burning Man 2010!

Tuesday, 12-2pm: Inside the Hookahdome: "Hookahdome is a chill lounge which incorporates flavored tabacco / shisha, tea and snack table service in an Arabic-themed, air-conditioned geodesic dome. Our guests lay back in Sultanic luxury and enjoy entertainment by our exquisite belly-dancers, acrobats, musicians and DJs while our lovely and attentive hospitality hosts & hostesses attend to their every want and need."


Wednesday, around sunrise: The False Profit Tuesday Party (located at 4:30 and Esplanade) is a Burning Man institution, and Dr. Toast is honored to continue his tradition of providing the soundtrack for the sunrise. Also, ALXNDR :: DJJD :: Dr. Toast :: Eugene Steele :: F’kir :: Ill Gates :: Kapt’n Kirk :: Lokae :: Mancub :: R/D :: Shipwreck :: and special guests. Sound provided by the Space Cowboys' Unimog.



Whenever: The Dr. is also expected to make spontaneous appearances on art cars such as The Jelly, The Phage, and The Janky Barge!
