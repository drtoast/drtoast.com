Friday, February 1, 2008 - Delicious Drips is a new company that brings "A unification of urban art vinyl toys that redefines the concept of the assembly line..." These toys are works of fine art rendered by local and international artists. Dr. Toast will be performing at their launch party along with Ganucheau, Cubik+Origami, Djeeno, DJ Squelchy, and DJ Daps. Reception featuring work by Coolvader, DLi$H, Boxguts, Phu!, & Tanki. Free sake bombs all night, free raffles every hour! 8pm til late, D-Structure, 520 Haight St, San Francisco
