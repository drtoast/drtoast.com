Dr. Toast makes his 4th annual performance at False Profit's Priceless campout this 4th of July weekend, at a beautiful location on the Feather River in the Sierra town of Belden, CA. Three days of camping and swimming plus interactive fire and video art from False Profit Labs, vegetarian/vegan food from Table Nectar, plus sculpture, workshops in balloon animals, hand percussion, knot tying, yoga, and staff spinning. Live music highlights include David Last, Deru, Mimosa, ganucheau, Genji, EPROM, Heyoka, Ill Gates, Kalpataru Tree, Mozaic, Random Rab, Welder, and many more. For more information, visit priceless.false-profit.com.
