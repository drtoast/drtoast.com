Saturday, April 12, 2008 - For the second year in a row, Dr. Toast has been invited to perform his music in a big ol' aircraft hangar at NASA! Yuri's Night is a celebration of the anniversary of the first human spaceflight with a fusion of art, science, music, and dance. The lineup also includes Amon Tobin, John Tejada, Lusine, Scuba, Digitonal vs Posthuman, Deru, Tycho, Random Rab, Mr. Projectile, Ganucheau, and more! More info, Tickets
