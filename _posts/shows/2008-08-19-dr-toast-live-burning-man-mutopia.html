Friday, August 30, 2008 @ 8pm: The Flaming Lotus Girls have outdone themselves again this year and created "Mutopia" - a fiery garden of metal made of cast aluminium, steel, copper, flame, and light, featuring 13 independent seed pods with computer-controlled propane flame effects. On the Friday night before the man burns, the Girls will host a fireworks show in front of Mutopia (located at 4:30 and Esplanade), and Dr. Toast has the honor of providing the soundtrack! After the fireworks there will be an intermission to clear the debris, then an all-night party. (Photo: David Glasser)
