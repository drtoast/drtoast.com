Friday, October 3, 2008, 4pm - 2am - Lepidodgera is the amazing giant multi-colored butterfly art car you might have seen at Burning Man this year. They're throwing a fundraiser at the Linden Street Brewery in Oakland this Friday - come meet the artists, enjoy a tasty beverage, and revel to the sounds of Dr. Toast, Nanda, Mimosa, Chlorophil, Dub Pirates, Tomas Cruzio, and Mihkal. 95 Linden St, Oakland, CA
