Thursday, June 10, 2010, 6pm-10pm - Dr. Toast performs in the aquarium at the California Academy of Sciences' NightLife series! Come to celebrate World Oceans Day and wander the halls and galleries of this amazing science museum in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park. Listen to music, enjoy a cocktail, and mingle with friends... Watch sustainable seafood cooking demonstrations by chefs from acclaimed San Francisco restaurants... See the unveiling of a new art project by The Crucible featuring copper and steel constructed over an ocean of beautifully colored glass... DJ MyKill will provide the music on the main floor, and Evolutionista and Dr. Toast will be providing the soundtrack in the aquarium. [event info, tickets, map]
