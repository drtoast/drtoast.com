Thursday, April 9, 2009, 6pm-10pm - Dr. Toast presents a fascinating lecture on the current state of Toastronomical Science at the California Academy of Sciences in Golden Gate Park, San Francisco. Well, either that, or he's spinning his toasty beats there for the 3rd annual Yuri's Night Bay Area as part of the Academy's NightLife series. Celebrate the anniversary of the first human spaceflight with music from Dr. Toast, Nalepa, Random Rab, and Cumulus, explore the indoor rain forest and Morrison Planetarium, hear lectures from visiting NASA scientists, and enjoy ambient soundscapes in the Steinhart Aquarium.
